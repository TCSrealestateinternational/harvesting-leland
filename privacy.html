Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Harvesting Leland ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit www.harvestingleland.com or interact with us.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address when you place an order, submit a form, or sign up for our newsletter.
  • Order information: Details about products you purchase or pre-order, including order history and payment information (processed securely through our payment provider — we do not store card numbers).
  • Communications: Messages you send us via contact or inquiry forms.
  • Usage data: General analytics data about how visitors interact with our website (page views, referral sources). This may be collected through tools like Google Analytics.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your orders
  • To communicate with you about your order, requests, or inquiries
  • To send marketing emails if you have opted in (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • To improve our website and customer experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information with trusted third-party service providers (such as payment processors, shipping carriers, or email marketing platforms) only as necessary to fulfill your order or provide our services. These providers are required to handle your information securely.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies — small text files stored on your browser — to improve your browsing experience and gather analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some site features may not function as intended.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. To make a request, please contact us at hello@harvestingleland.com.

Data Retention

We retain customer information for as long as necessary to fulfill orders and comply with legal requirements. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us directly.

Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
