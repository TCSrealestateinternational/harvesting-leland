Pre-Sale Information

A pre-sale means you order your items before we make them. Everything at Harvesting Leland is crafted fresh in small batches — we don't keep inventory sitting on a shelf. Your order is made specifically for you after we receive it, which ensures maximum freshness and quality.
Pre-sale orders for baked goods close every Friday at 5:00 PM. Orders placed after the deadline will roll into the following week's batch. We'll always confirm your order and the pickup/delivery date by email.
Our baked goods menu rotates seasonally and sometimes weekly depending on what's available from the farm and garden. The best way to stay current is to follow us on Instagram or join our email list — we announce the weekly menu there first.
Absolutely! Use the Request an Item form to let us know what you're looking for. We'll do our best to accommodate and will reach out to confirm before production.
We can often accommodate specific needs like dairy-free, nut-free, or reduced-sugar options. Please reach out via the Connect page before ordering to discuss your needs, and we'll let you know what's possible.

Shipping & Local Pickup

Yes! Local pickup is our preferred method for baked goods and perishable items. Once your order is confirmed, we'll provide specific pickup instructions. See the "Visiting Us" section below for location details.
Currently, baked goods are local pickup only due to freshness. Non-perishable items — including home goods, herbal products, and wellness items — can be shipped. Shipping details and rates will be provided at checkout. We ship within the continental U.S.
Local delivery may be available in certain circumstances for larger orders. Please reach out via the Contact page to inquire.

Pre-Order Timelines

For events, we recommend ordering at least 2–3 weeks in advance, especially for larger quantities or custom items. This allows us to plan ingredients, schedule baking time, and ensure everything is perfect for your occasion.
Our sourdough loaves stay fresh for 3–5 days at room temperature (in a bread bag or wrapped in a cloth) or up to 3 months in the freezer. Most baked goods are best enjoyed within 2–3 days of pickup. We'll always include storage instructions with your order.
Most herbal and home goods are ready to ship within 5–7 business days. Custom or made-to-order knit items may require additional time — we'll always give you an accurate estimate when you order.

Local Visit Info

Yes! We periodically appear at local markets and events. Follow us on Instagram or sign up for our email list to be the first to know where you can find us in person.

General Questions

We are not certified organic, but we are deeply intentional about our ingredients. We grow what we can without pesticides, and we source from farms and suppliers we trust. If you have questions about specific ingredients, just ask — we're always happy to share.
Absolutely — our products make beautiful, thoughtful gifts. We can add simple gift wrapping or a handwritten note on request. Mention it in the notes when you order, or use the Request an Item form for larger gift bundles.
Because our baked goods are made fresh to order, we are unable to offer refunds on food items once baked. If there is an issue with your order, please contact us within 24 hours of pickup and we'll make it right. For non-perishable goods, we accept returns within 14 days if the item is unused and in original condition. See our Terms of Service for full details.
The best ways to stay in the loop: follow us on Instagram for real-time updates, and sign up for our email list (in the footer of any page) for weekly pre-sale announcements and new product drops.
We'd love to hear from you! Head to the Contact page and send us a message. We typically respond within 1–2 business days.
